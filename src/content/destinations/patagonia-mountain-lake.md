## Why It Is Beautiful

Patagonia is all scale and weather: pale blue glaciers, hard granite skylines, bright lakes, and open valleys that make every walk feel cinematic. The beauty is not polished; it is raw, immense, and constantly changing.

## Practical Travel Notes

Give yourself buffer days. Flights, ferries, and trail plans can all be affected by weather. Book popular routes early in high season, and keep a flexible layer system ready for wind, rain, and sun in the same afternoon.
