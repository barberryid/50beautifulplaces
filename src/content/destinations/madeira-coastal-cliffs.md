## Why It Is Beautiful

Madeira feels carved upward from the Atlantic. The coastline is dramatic, but the interior is just as memorable: misty laurel forest, stone levadas, terraced villages, and ridgelines where the clouds move below your feet.

## Practical Travel Notes

Base yourself in Funchal for the easiest mix of restaurants, bus access, and day trips. Rent a car if you want sunrise hikes or quieter coastal viewpoints. Mountain weather changes quickly, so pack layers even when the coast is warm.
