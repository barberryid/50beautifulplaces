## Why It Is Beautiful

The South Coast compresses many of Iceland's most striking landscapes into one route. Water, ice, volcanic sand, and green cliffs sit close together, giving short drives an outsized sense of discovery.

## Practical Travel Notes

Wind and road conditions matter. Check official alerts before driving, keep distance from sneaker waves on black-sand beaches, and book accommodation early in summer.
