## Why It Is Beautiful

Namibia's desert landscapes are spare, graphic, and unforgettable. The distances are part of the experience: long roads, huge horizons, and sudden moments of color where dunes, rock, and sky meet.

## Practical Travel Notes

Plan fuel, water, and daylight carefully. Many roads are gravel, so drive conservatively and check rental conditions. Camps and lodges near major landmarks should be booked ahead during dry-season travel.
